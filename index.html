Why HR buyers care

This tool helps managers make faster, more consistent decisions while reducing HR risk. It creates structure, improves documentation, and helps ensure issues are escalated appropriately before they become bigger problems.

  • Reduce inconsistency across managers and departments
  • Catch leave, complaint, retaliation, and protected-risk issues earlier
  • Give managers a better first screen before HR gets pulled in
This tool provides general HR guidance based on common practices. It does not replace your company’s policies, HR review, or legal counsel. Final employment decisions should always be reviewed through the appropriate internal HR, leadership, and legal process.
